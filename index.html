    • Introduction:

    Blender is one of the best free 3D softwares at the moment, and is growing along with the community that supports it.

    We are going see how we can get started with blender as a bigginer in 2023.

    Installation:

    Blender is a free 3D software and you can get it just about anywhere. For some you get just get it straight from your Microsoft Store, for most you can download it from the Blender website on your browser.

    What you get:

    As mentioned before. Blender has been and continues to be one of the most powerful open source tools in the Film, animation and Graphic Design industry.

    With Blender you will be able to do:

  • 3D Modeling & Skulpting.
  • Create and Animate Environment/Assets ready for Game Development.
  • Rigging Character in both 2D and 3D space.
  • Video Editing.
  • Compositing.
  • Visual Effects and more.

    • Basically you will be able to do all your work in one software most of the time, and not having to jump from software to software doing one project.

    Blender:

    As mentioned above blender has a verity of tools to help you with optimal workflow.

    When you open the software you will be able to choose from the get go, the different workspaces you would prefer to work in, like the 2D workspace (also known as Grease Pencil) or the General workspace which is the 3D Animation interface or just Modeling and Editing.

    Pros and Cons:

    There are many good things blender can do

  • You can work on a large project and finish them in one go "that is if you machine can hendle the pressure."
  • You will be part of a community that's always available and ready to help you with your blender experience.
  • And lastly It's free!
    • However there are some downside.

  • Even though it is a free software, you will find that you need to buy some Add-ons for workflow to be faster unless you prefere modeling and builder your own assets, you would also have to credit the original creators of some of the models you might use from the add-ons.
  • You might sometimes feel the need to use some paid softwares like Adobe Premiere or After-Effect for better effects and so on...

    • If you would like to download the Blender software or you would like to know more info about blender and everything discussed in this document feel free to visit the Blender website
